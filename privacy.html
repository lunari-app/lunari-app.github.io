Introduction

This Privacy Policy sets out how the app Lunari uses and protects your personal data that you generate and store within the app.

Data Use

Lunari respects and celebrates your privacy. There is no collection of usage data or personal information, no ads, no spyware. Lunari can store data related to your menstrual health locally on your phone, and this includes:

  • Period start and end dates
  • Cycle length and period duration
  • Symptoms and mood tracking data
  • Notes and personal observations
  • App preferences and settings

This data is used solely to provide you with period predictions, cycle insights, and personalized tracking features. We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal health data to any third parties.

If you wish to delete all your app data, you can do so by going to the settings menu and selecting "Delete data". This can also be done by uninstalling the app.

Permissions

Lunari may request the following permissions:

  • Reminders: To send you period reminders and cycle predictions.
  • Biometric authentication: To secure your app with fingerprint or face recognition (optional).

If you don't want to use any of these permissions, you can revoke them at any time through your device settings.

Transparency

We believe in complete transparency about how your data is handled:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • You can delete all your data at any time
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • No third-party advertising or data collection

You have full control over your data. You can delete all tracking data through the settings menu, and the app will continue to work normally without any stored information.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at lunari.appmail@gmail.com.
